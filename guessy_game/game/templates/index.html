How to Play

I am thinking of a 3-digit number with no repeated digits. Try guessing what it is.

Here are the clues:

  • Chop_rice: One digit is correct but in the wrong position.
  • Woza: One digit is correct and in the right position.
  • Yooh: No digit is correct.

Example: If the secret number was 123 and your guess was 421, the program would print: Woza Chop_rice.
